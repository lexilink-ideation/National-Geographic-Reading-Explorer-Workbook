3. Where did Finkbeiner and his classmates plant the first tree?

Answer: Outside their school.

4. How many members does Plant-for-the-Planet have?

Answer: Around 70,000 members.

5. What is the goal now?

Answer: One trillion trees - 150 for every person on Earth.
